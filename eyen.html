Project description

The European Youth Energy Network (EYEN) is the think-tank placing youth at the heart of the European energy transition. They believe in a future where the European youth is instrumental in shaping a just and sustainable energy transition. To achieve this, EYEN aims to build intergenerational trust, empower young people with the necessary knowledge and skills, and create platforms for them to take action. EYEN unites youth-led organisations in Europe with a focus on energy-related initiatives.

EYEN Members are non-profit, fully established European energy-focused youth organisations that support EYEN's vision. As an European federation, their main goal is to unify the youth movement that takes place through youth organizations and echo their efforts in accelerating the energy transition. Their voice only gets stronger by gathering as many youth organizations as possible, showing that their vision for a better energy system in Europe is a priority at regional, national and European level. By helping their members stand together we prove that youth opinions should be considered in decision making processes.
